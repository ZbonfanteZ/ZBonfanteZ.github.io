Zoe Bonfante

Zoe Bonfante is an actress and singer who lives in Marin County. She has been training since she caught her love for the art during covid. From martial arts to music, she has it all covered.

Skills

  • Fluent in French
  • Singer
  • Dancer
  • Scuba Diver
  • Musician
  • Athlete

Zoe has been featured as the lead in muiltiple theater productions including Newsies and Mean Girls as well as in a few short films that were screened at the Cannes Film Festival.
